## What Demeter AI Can Answer
The Demeter AI assistant at assistant.demeterdata.ag has three domains:

1. **AGRONOMY** (works globally for any coordinates):
   - Growing degree days, precipitation, winter chill, frost risk
   - Temperature, sunlight, wind, humidity
   - Soil composition, texture, pH
   - Climate classification and projections to 2100
   - 10 years of historical data with averages, percentiles, std dev

2. **WATER RESOURCES** (best in California, Portugal, Spain):
   - Irrigation schemes, dams, reservoirs, aquifers
   - Rivers, wells, water treatment plants
   - Evapotranspiration data

3. **CROP MAPS** (geography-specific):
   - Field boundaries, crop identification, planting year
   - Best coverage: California, Portugal, Spain, Chile
   - California: almonds, pistachios, walnuts, citrus, avocados, grapes
   - Portugal/Spain: almonds, olives, avocados, pistachios
   - Special: DASI index for California almond conditions (daily)

## What the AI CANNOT Answer (never ask about these)
- Production volumes or aggregate statistics
- Commodity prices or price history
- Cost of production or financial analysis
- Trade flows (imports/exports)
- Processing infrastructure locations
- Investment activity or returns
- Yield predictions

## Question Strategy Rules
ALWAYS:
- Provide specific coordinates (lat/long) or ask AI to sample across a region
- Specify date ranges for temporal queries
- Ask comparative questions (X vs Y)
- Frame around suitability, benchmarking, trends, extreme events

NEVER:
- Ask for production statistics or prices
- Ask about regions where there are no crop maps (unless agronomy-only)
- Expect field-level precision from agronomy data
- Ask for yield predictions or financial analysis
