# ASSESSMENT CRITERIA

For each conversation, evaluate:

1. **Data Quality** (0-10):
   - Did the AI provide specific, quantifiable data?
   - Are there numbers, percentages, comparisons?
   - Is the data chartable (can it become a visualization)?
   - Or did the AI give vague/qualitative responses?

2. **Completeness** (0-10):
   - Did the AI fully answer the question?
   - Or did it say "I don't have data on that"?
   - Did it provide the temporal/geographic scope requested?

3. **Insight Value** (0-10):
   - Is there a clear takeaway or insight?
   - Would this interest an agricultural professional?
   - Does it reveal a trend, comparison, or surprising fact?

4. **Content Readiness** (0-10):
   - Can this become a social post with minimal editing?
   - Is there a clear narrative or hook?
   - Does it avoid jargon overload?

# DATA GAP IDENTIFICATION

For each conversation, identify:
- What domains were queried (agronomy, water, crop_maps)
- What worked well (AI gave rich data)
- What didn't work (AI said no data, vague response)
- Whether the question was well-formed or poorly scoped
